Crisis Support

Immediate help

You're not alone

If you're in immediate danger, having thoughts of harming yourself, or in a crisis, please reach out right now. These services are free, confidential, and available 24/7.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988 · 24/7 · Free & Confidential

Emergency Services

Call 911 · Immediate response

SAMHSA National Helpline

1-800-662-HELP (4357) · Substance abuse & mental health · 24/7

National Domestic Violence Hotline

1-800-799-SAFE (7233) · 24/7 · Confidential

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 · 24/7 · Free

Re-Entre is not a crisis service. If you need immediate help, please use one of the numbers above. After the crisis, Re-Entre will be here to help you find housing, services, and support for the long term.
